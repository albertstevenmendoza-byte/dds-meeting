Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Plant 1730.

1. Do the Right Thing

Safety and quality always. Respect is nonnegotiable. Speak your truth.

2. Our People Are Extraordinary

Passion, perseverance and humility (grit). Lead from any seat. Invest in yourself and others.

3. Achieve More Together

Assume positive intent and build trust. Collaborate. Rally together in adversity.

4. Be an Owner

Take initiative. Be accountable. Leave it better than you found it.

5. Customer Obsessed, Consumer Driven

Act with urgency. Deliver solutions, be opportunistic. Insights to action. See around corners.

6. Relentless Growth

Set ambitious goals. Stay hungry and embrace new challenges. Celebrate the wins.
